About Me

Hello, I’m Md. Tanvir Alam Sifat. I’m passionate about leveraging technology to drive social impact and financial inclusion. My professional journey spans software architecture, digital product development, project management and research in areas such as ICT4D (Information and Communication Technologies for Development), digital financial services and AI for social good.
As a manager in microfinance technology at BRAC I lead the development and deployment of enterprise software that serves millions of clients and thousands of staff. I previously managed SaaS products at Digicon Technologies and have hands‑on experience designing mobile, web and IoT solutions. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science and continuously up‑skill through certifications in business analysis, project management and digital finance.
Beyond work, I actively engage with community and professional organisations, mentor aspiring technologists and advocate for responsible innovation.
